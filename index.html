About Us

Houston's Renovation Company of Choice

Houstonian Home Renovations has been proudly serving the greater Houston, TX community with unmatched home construction and remodeling craftsmanship for over 30 years. Whether you’re building a new home from scratch or completely renovating an existing structure, we will work closely with you to help you make the best selections for your budget, keep you informed of the project’s progress, and ensure your vision for your home is ultimately achieved.

Our team of construction experts pride themselves on providing you with every service your project requires. From architectural design and planning to material and product selection to post-construction cleaning, we’ll make your remodeling experience with us a smooth, efficient, and painless one without any hidden costs or delays.
